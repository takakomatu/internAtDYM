A vaccination can not only prevent the outbreak of an infectious disease, but a child who receives the vaccination may not get ill,or stay with minimum sickness. In Hong-Kong, you can receive the same treatment as in Japan. However, consult with your doctor about Allergies prior to vaccination.

Difference between vaccination in Japan, and Hong-Kong for Children

One of which many families face, is the problem of vaccination overseas. Periodic inoculation in Japan is different in terms of types and timing relative to global standards. And this brings confusion to families overseas.

There are no right answers of vaccination schedules depending on giving birth in Japan and moving to Hong-Kong, or giving birth outside of Japan and moving to Hong-Kong. However this page will discuss case scenarios including different vaccination types, schedules, and preventable diseases.
